## The proposition

*Life is the centre. Love is the whole.*

A direct counter to anthropocentrism: not the human at the centre, but life.
Not the individual as the unit, but love — connection, relation, care — as
the whole.

## Qualia and quanta

Our age is defined by the quantification of everything: efficiency,
optimisation, economic value, data. Qualis insists that the *qualia* — the
felt qualities of experience, the texture of being alive — are equally real
and equally necessary. Together, quanta and qualia form the whole.

The name: *qualis* (Latin: of what kind, of what quality).

## The tetrahedral structure

Buckminster Fuller: the tetrahedron is the minimum system in Universe.
Four vertices, six edges — **four entities and six relationships**.
The relationships outnumber the entities. Connection is prior to the node.

Two intersecting tetrahedra describe the whole sphere.

In the Qualis model, **life is the shared node** — the centre where the
two tetrahedra meet:

```
Quanta (physical substrate):   time · energy · matter
                                         ↕
                                       LIFE
                                         ↕
Qualia (metaphysical experience): body · mind · spirit
```

The solar system as celestial metaphor: Sun / Moon / Earth = mind / spirit / body.
Universe expresses the whole in fractal representations throughout its
physical structure and metaphysical being.

## In-ness and out-ness

Fuller's fundamental distinction:

- **Out-ness** — physical, objective, quantitative materiality. What we see
  is light.
- **In-ness** — metaphysical, subjective, qualitative experience. What we
  don't see is attraction.

The geometry of spheres (vector equilibrium, closest-packing) provides the
physical metaphor for the metaphysical.

## Love as metaphysical gravity

From Synergetics 543.20–22:

> "The scientific word for the integral of all the special case realizations
> of gravity is love."
>
> "Love is metaphysical gravity."

Fuller is not using "love" as metaphor. He is saying gravity and love are
the same comprehensive embracement at different scales of description —
the universe's tendency toward coherence rather than dispersal, experienced
from the inside at the scale of conscious life.

## Time: chronos and kairos

**Chronos** — quantitative, sequential, measurable. Time as container.
**Kairos** — qualitative, opportune, meaningful. Time as opening.

Both are real. The solar year becomes the daily metaphor for life and breath.
An annual meditation on chronos/kairos time developed in parallel with Jenny
Odell's *Saving Time*. Steven Johnson's *adjacent possible* sits in the same
neighbourhood — a kairos concept in scientific language, about readiness and
relation, not just sequence.

## The Universal Coordinate System

`coord.qualis.life` — a geometric/interactive expression of the model.
Maps the full structure: in-ness and out-ness, the tetrahedral integration
of qualia and quanta, orientation within the Qualis conceptual space.

## Relationship to ARC

Qualis is the *why*. ARC is the *how*. ARC provides the sovereignty stack
that lets life-centred content exist without capture by CMS-as-silo or
surveillance capitalism. The Qualis cluster (`qualis.life`) is a set of ARC
contexts — it needs its own context definitions and, eventually, its own
design system (no equivalent to Holobiont yet).
